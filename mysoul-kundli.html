✦ Dosh in Your Chart?

Been Told You Have a Dosh?
Here's What Your Report Covers.

Here's what your Premium report addresses for the three most commonly misunderstood ones.

🪐
Saturn Transit
Sade Sati

Saturn's 7.5-year transit across your moon sign. Widely feared, consistently misunderstood. Your report explains which of the three phases you're currently in — rising, peak, or setting — what it means for your career, health, and relationships, and specific remedies for your chart.

🐍
Rahu–Ketu Axis
Kaal Sarp Dosh

All planets between Rahu and Ketu. Your report clarifies whether your chart actually has it, how severe the effect is in your specific houses, and how to work with the energy rather than against it.

🔥
Mars Placement
Mangal Dosh

The most misrepresented dosh in Vedic astrology. Most people with Mangal Dosh lead completely normal relationship lives — because the actual impact depends on your specific Mars house, cancellations present, and your partner's chart.

A dosh is not a verdict. It is a placement. Understanding it — specifically, in your chart — changes everything.

No. Our team manually studies your chart and writes your report from it — that's why it takes 24–48 hours, not seconds.

We need your full name, date of birth, birth time (approximate is fine), and birth city. If you don't know the exact time, mention morning/afternoon/evening.
What if I don't know my exact birth time? ▾
No problem. Share the closest approximation. Many important sections don't require an exact birth time. We clearly indicate what's affected.

Free apps show raw data — planetary positions, house numbers, dasha dates. This report is the interpretation: what your career timing, relationship patterns, and wealth energy mean for your actual life right now.
